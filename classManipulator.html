Creates a circle around a given axis and returns its vertices.

Parameters
[in]_segmentsA larger amount of segments creates a smoother circle.
[in]_thicknessGreatly chnages how visible and easy to click the circle is.
[in]_rThe distance (radius) of the circle from the op point.
[in]_axisAxis around the circle is made. Use ROTATION_X, ROTATION_Y or ROTATION_Z.
[in]_numbIf more than one circle is needed, you can enter either 0 or 1.
[in]_opThe point the circle should be made around. Should usually be origin (0,0,0).
[out]_verticesA vector of all the vertex positions.
